What’s Bool?

Meet Bool, your child’s future companion, mentor, and friend. We understand the unique challenges faced by parents of autistic children and the crucial role of support, education, and companionship in their development. That’s why we’ve created Bool – a revolutionary companion robot designed with your child’s well-being in mind.

Why Bool?

Bool is not just a robot; it’s a friend. Its name, pronounced like “ball,” reflects its spherical design and approachable nature. Bool is here to provide a world of possibilities for your child:

  • Companionship: Bool is your child’s constant companion, offering comfort and friendship whenever needed.

  • Rehabilitation Training: With professional rehabilitation programs tailored to your child’s unique needs, Bool assists in achieving developmental milestones.

  • Educational Support: Bool is equipped to help your child with school curriculum tasks, making learning engaging and enjoyable.

  • Emotional Connection: Bool is designed to understand and respond to your child’s emotions, fostering a sense of trust and security.

Join the Bool Revolution

We invite you to be a part of the Bool revolution. If you’re interested in providing your child with the support and companionship they deserve, please leave your email and credit card information below. Rest assured, your information will be kept secure and used exclusively for notifying you about Bool’s development progress and availability.






We Care About Your Privacy

We take your privacy seriously. Your data is important to us, and we will handle it with the utmost care. We will only use your information to keep you informed about Bool’s journey. You can trust us to respect your privacy.

Bool is not just a robot; it’s a promise of support, education, and friendship for your child. Join us in making a difference in your child’s life. Together, we can unlock a brighter future.
